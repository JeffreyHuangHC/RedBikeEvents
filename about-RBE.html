Red Bike Events

When his new red bicycle was stolen, Muhammad Ali filed a report to a policeman and vowed to punish the thief. As a part-time boxing coach alongside his usual occupation, the policeman invited Ali to a local boxing club. Never did they manage to capture the thief, but the theft itself catalyzed the fruition of something else: a boxing career for Ali that brought communities together, one at a time.

Inspired by Ali's stolen red bicycle, we set out on our own mission to further unite communities in celebration and support of local talent.
